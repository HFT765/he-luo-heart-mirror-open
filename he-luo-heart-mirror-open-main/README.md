**HeLuo Heart Mirror** is an open-source framework for civilizational awakening, grounded in the ancient wisdom of the **Hetu and Luoshu** diagrams.

**Origin**: The project originated from a simple idea about healthâ€”to design tools that remind people of natural bio-rhythms. This led us back to the traditional Chinese medicine concept of "midday-midnight ebb and flow," and further, to the foundational system that has undergirded Chinese civilization for millennia: the Hetu and Luoshu. We regard them as the **"open-source protocol"** and **"core algorithms"** of a cultural operating system.

**Mission**: Our goal is to make this profound wisdom **modern, systematic, and open for collaboration**. We aim to:
1.  **Reinterpret Classics**: Provide contemporary explanations for ancient texts like the *I Ching*, *Tao Te Ching*, and *Yellow Emperor's Canon*.
2.  **Build Frameworks**: Develop clear frameworks for personal cultivation (the Ten States of the Hetu) and social practice (the Nine-Dimensional Model of the Luoshu).
3.  **Provide Tools**: Create practical resources such as visual models, self-assessment tools, and community guides.

**Vision**: We aspire to co-create an open ecosystem of wisdom with global developers, scholars, and practitioners. Our aim is to offer an **"open-source solution"** for **personal transformation** and **civilizational renewal**, rooted in Eastern thought and accessible for all to use, adapt, and improve.

- **Volume I: The Essence** - Ten Life States of the Hetu

- **Volume II: The Application** - Nine-Dimensional Practice Framework of the Luoshu
  Using the Nine Palaces of the Luoshu as a blueprint, it provides practical methods across nine dimensions of social practice, from "Establishing the Central Core" to "Illuminating Civilization."

- **Volume III: The Integration** - Unified Practice System of Hetu & Luoshu
  Integrates the "Essence" of the Hetu and the "Application" of the Luoshu across ten unified practices like "Integration of Number and Symbol" and "Integration of Dao and Technology," pointing toward the path of a harmonious world.
